OBJECTIVES OF THE FESTIVAL

The first festival dedicated to Kosovar composers is a historic debut in Kosovo's cultural scene, serving as an important platform to honor, preserve, and promote Kosovo's rich musical heritage. This distinguished event is designed as a series of activities spanning a year, with a clear goal: creating, disseminating, and protecting musical works. Organized for the first time, this festival declares the need to embark on a significant journey towards promoting Kosovar composers and presenting them on the Kosovar stage.

Starting from the lack of space and expressive opportunities, this organization aims to create an inspiring and enriching environment for new artistic creations in Kosovo's music.
